Cookie Policy

Last updated: 10 July 2026

Cookies and similar technologies (like your browser's local storage) let a website remember things between visits. ThaiThuk keeps them to a minimum and only turns on advertising cookies if you agree.

Strictly necessary (always on)

A small amount of local storage on your own device remembers your theme (light/dark), your cookie choice, and your quiz progress and any forum posts. This never leaves your device and isn't sent to a server. The site can't work sensibly without it, so it's always on.

Advertising (off until you accept)

If you accept advertising, ads are served by Google AdSense, which may set cookies (including the DoubleClick/DART cookie) to display and measure ads and to show ads based on your visits to this and other sites. Until you accept, no advertising scripts request ads. You can review how Google uses cookies in advertising and opt out via Google Ads Settings.

Analytics

We do not currently run third-party analytics or tracking pixels. If that ever changes, we'll update this policy and ask for consent where the law requires it.

Managing your choices

You're in control. Open at any time to turn advertising on or off. You can also clear cookies and local storage through your browser's settings, and most browsers let you block cookies entirely.
