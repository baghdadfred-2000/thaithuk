Cookie Policy

Last updated: 7 July 2026

What we store

Essential local storage (no consent needed): your dark/light theme choice (thaituk_theme) and demo forum posts (thaituk_forum_v1) live only in your browser. They contain no tracking identifiers and are never sent to our servers.

Third-party cookies

If advertising (e.g., Google AdSense) or embedded content is enabled, those providers may set cookies for ad delivery and measurement. Where required by law, a consent banner will ask before any non-essential cookies are set. See Google's cookie policy for details of their practices.

Managing cookies

You can clear or block cookies and site data in your browser settings at any time. Clearing site data will reset your theme preference and demo forum posts.
