Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 July 2026

ThaiThuk.com ("we", "us") is an informational magazine website. We collect as little personal data as possible.

What we collect

Newsletter: if you subscribe, we store your email address solely to send the newsletter. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in every email. Contact form: messages you send us, including your name and email, are used only to respond to you. Local storage: your theme preference (dark/light) and demo forum posts are stored in your own browser and never transmitted to us.

Analytics and advertising

We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand aggregate readership. If we display advertising (e.g., Google AdSense), the ad provider may use cookies as described in our Cookie Policy; consent is requested where required by law.

What we don't do

We do not sell personal data, run tracking beyond the services named above, or require accounts to read the site.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold (typically just a newsletter email) by writing to privacy@thaituk.com. We honour applicable rights under Thailand's PDPA, the GDPR and similar laws.
