Commitment

Accessibility

Last updated: 10 July 2026

We want everyone to be able to read and enjoy ThaiThuk — whoever you are and however you browse. We aim to meet the spirit of the WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines and keep improving.

What we've built in

  • Keyboard navigation throughout, with visible focus outlines.
  • Semantic headings and landmarks, plus ARIA labels on menus, the search box, the quiz and forms.
  • Colour contrast aimed at WCAG AA for body text, and a light/dark theme you control.
  • Meaning never carried by colour alone — icons and text labels back it up.
  • Large touch targets and a responsive layout that reflows cleanly down to small phones.
  • Respect for your prefers-reduced-motion setting, so animations ease off if you've asked your device to reduce motion.
  • Descriptive alternative text on meaningful images.

Third-party content

Some content we don't fully control — most notably the advertising served by Google AdSense — may not always meet the same accessibility standards. You can switch advertising off at any time in , which keeps those third-party frames from loading.

Still not right for you?

Accessibility is never "done." If anything here is hard to use with assistive technology, please tell us and we'll fix it — use our Contact page, and let us know the page and what got in your way.
